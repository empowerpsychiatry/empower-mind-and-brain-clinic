About Dr. Sanjana Ilavarasu

Dr. Sanjana Ilavarasu is a compassionate and dedicated psychiatrist with a deep commitment to mental health and holistic well-being. With years of clinical experience and a strong academic foundation, she combines evidence-based treatments with a personalized approach to help individuals navigate their mental health journeys.

She completed her postgraduate training in psychiatry and has worked extensively with patients across various age groups, addressing a wide range of concerns including anxiety, depression, stress-related disorders, relationship challenges, and severe mental illnesses. Her therapeutic approach is rooted in empathy, respect, and collaboration—ensuring that every patient feels heard, understood, and empowered.

She believes in breaking the stigma surrounding mental health and is passionate about raising awareness, offering psychoeducation, and fostering resilience in her patients. At her clinic, Dr. Sanjana strives to create a safe, non-judgmental space where healing can begin and individuals can rediscover their strength.
